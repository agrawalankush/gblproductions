About GBL Productions

GBL Productions is a Bangalore-based independent music label dedicated to creating authentic, original, and AI-free music. Founded by Kartik Mishra, GBL Productions is the creative culmination of a deep passion for music, storytelling, and collaboration. As a proudly homegrown label, we specialize in end-to-end song creation—right from conceptualizing melodies and lyrics to full-scale production, mixing, and mastering.

At the heart of GBL Productions is a commitment to genuine human creativity. In an era where artificial intelligence is increasingly used in music generation, we take a principled stand to keep our artform human-led, emotional, and organic. Every track we produce reflects the raw, unfiltered talent of the artists behind it.

Our mission extends beyond just producing music—we strive to be a nurturing ground for young and talented music producers. By offering them real-world opportunities to work on our compositions, we help cultivate fresh voices in the Indian music landscape. Alongside our in-house work, we actively collaborate with respected production entities like Dream Art Records (Assam) and Bassleela Music (Delhi) to push the boundaries of creative exchange and regional diversity.

With a focus on quality, originality, and artistic integrity, GBL Productions is emerging as a bold and independent voice in India’s evolving music industry.
