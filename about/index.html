About Kartik Mishra

Kartik Mishra is a multifaceted artist who seamlessly blends romantic and pop music with a hint of rock cult influence. Known for his poetic and deep writing style, Kartik’s songs explore the emotional complexities of love and life. His sound combines melodious tunes with a raw edge, capturing listeners' hearts with his soul-stirring vocals and heartfelt lyrics.

Based out of Bengaluru, Kartik's roots lie in Patna, where his journey in music began alongside his technical expertise. A software engineer by education, he brings a unique perspective to music production, utilizing advanced tools like Ableton to craft his sound. His music reflects not only his creative depth but also his technical proficiency in the digital realm.

GBL Productions Vision

GBL Productions - is a Bangalore-based independent music label dedicated to creating authentic, original, and AI-free music. GBL Productions is the creative culmination of a deep passion for music, storytelling, and collaboration. As a proudly homegrown label, we specialize in end-to-end song creation—right from conceptualizing melodies and lyrics to full-scale production, mixing, and mastering.

At the heart of GBL Productions is a commitment to genuine human creativity. In an era where artificial intelligence is increasingly used in music generation, we take a principled stand to keep our artform human-led, emotional, and organic. Every track we produce reflects the raw, unfiltered talent of the artists behind it.
